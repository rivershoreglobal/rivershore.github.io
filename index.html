About

I’m Ian Crowther, a Data & AI leader who grew from an accomplished Node.js engineer into a strategist and team builder. I’ve led modernisation programmes, unified data across domains, and embedded data-as-a-product thinking so teams can deliver trusted insights and AI applications quickly and safely.

AI for workforce efficiency: I deploy practical AI tooling (LLM copilots, semantic layers, retrieval patterns, governed access) so engineers, analysts, and product teams deliver faster with higher quality—safely and compliantly.

I’ve orchestrated single sources of truth, fostered collaboration between data producers and consumers, and built forums to manage cross-domain dependencies effectively. My leadership spans globally distributed teams across Portugal, Romania, Poland, Ukraine, UK, USA, and Brazil.

Today, I focus on:

  • Data Foundations – finops, platform, pipelines, quality, lineage, governance, and catalog.
  • Data Pillars – dataops, data platform, data engineering, analytics engineering, feature stores, machine learning, and generative AI.
  • Enablement – self-service access, semantic layers, and AI-ready design.
