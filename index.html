About

Engineer-turned data leader delivering 60% faster cycles and 30% lower cost.

I’m Ian Crowther, a Data & AI leader who grew from Node.js engineering into strategy and team building. I modernise legacy estates into governed, product-led, AI-ready platforms—unifying data across domains and embedding Data-as-a-Product so teams deliver trusted insights and AI quickly and safely. I scale teams and operating models to compound productivity gains. I’ve built single sources of truth, aligned data producers and consumers, and established mechanisms to manage cross-domain dependencies across globally distributed teams.

I build trust in data by ensuring quality at source (100% test coverage for critical pipelines and clear ownership); enforcing SOX-compliant change and incident processes (≈20% fewer incidents); improving reliability through monitoring, alerting and SLOs that enhance MTTD/MTTR and reduce escalations; and providing governed access with role-based controls, lineage and catalog for auditability and safe self-service.

  • Data Foundations — FinOps, platform, pipelines, quality, lineage, governance, catalog.
  • Data Pillars — DataOps, data platform, data engineering, analytics engineering, feature stores, ML & generative AI.

Outcomes Delivered

  • Hyper-growth — Scaled the organisation from 12 → 110+ in four years; authored the career framework (levels, competencies, progression criteria) and served as a mentor so people knew how to progress, driving internal promotions and strengthening culture & retention. ,
  • Data Estate Modernisation — Migrated Python pipelines to Databricks, cutting cycle time 60% and infrastructure cost 30%, enabling faster delivery and a lower TCO.
  • Platform Reliability — Implemented end-to-end monitoring and alerting with clear SLOs/SLAs, improving MTTD/MTTR with ~20% fewer incidents; authored the support & incident management process to standardise response and recovery.
  • Governance — Established ownership, lineage, access controls, and quality policies to increase stakeholder trust and auditability, reducing escalations and enabling safer change.
  • Data as a Product — Delivered a Single Source of Truth across domains, driving ~$8–10m productivity gains by eliminating analyst-side rework (no double-handling of transformations) and removing duplicate definitions, resulting in a 40% reduction in support/reporting overhead.
  • AI Enablement — LLM-assisted model & query generation plus quality guardrails delivered ~40% faster pipelines, fewer defects, and 100% test coverage.
  • FinOps — Cost transparency enabling better budgeting and cost-aware decisions across teams, improving forecast accuracy and spend accountability.
